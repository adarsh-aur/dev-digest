## Summary

Formspree's free plan does not include spam filtering, allowing spam submissions to reach the inbox,A test was conducted with identical contact forms on Formspree and Formgrid, submitting a real spam email to both,The spam email was not filtered by Formspree and reached the inbox, while Formgrid silently blocked the submission,Formgrid detected multiple red flags in the submission, including emoji in the name field, cryptocurrency content, and disposable email address,Formgrid's spam protection blocked the submission before it reached the inbox, preventing email notification and lead creation,The lack of spam filtering on Formspree's free plan can be disruptive for teams and businesses, especially those with shared inboxes,Formgrid's automatic spam detection and blocking can help prevent unwanted content from reaching the inbox
