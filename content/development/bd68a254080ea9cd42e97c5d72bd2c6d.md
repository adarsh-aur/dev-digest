## Summary

Solana accounts are analogous to database rows,Primary keys in databases are similar to public keys or PDAs in Solana,Schema in databases is equivalent to program logic in Solana,Data is stored on a shared network, not a single company server,Program logic determines data meaning and access permissions,Accounts are tied to identity and data, with unique behavior,Solana focuses on ownership, permissions, and state rather than traditional tables
