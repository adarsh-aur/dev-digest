## Summary

Inaccessible websites create barriers for users with disabilities,Accessibility is not just a legal requirement, but an opportunity to reach more customers,Choosing an accessible theme is crucial, such as Storefront, a WooCommerce-friendly theme,Testing tools like WAVE, Equalize Digital Accessibility Checker, NVDA, and VoiceOver can help identify issues,Proper heading structure is essential for screen reader users, with one H1 per page and no skipped levels,Clear link text is necessary, avoiding vague phrases like 'Click here' or 'Learn more',Accessibility is an ongoing process, not a one-time checklist
